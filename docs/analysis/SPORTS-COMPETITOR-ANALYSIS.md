# PLANOVO — Competitor Analysis: Sports Section Scheduling & Management Software

## Executive Summary

The sports section management software market is fragmented between **Western platforms** (too expensive, not localized for CIS) and **Russian CRMs** (built for beauty salons, not sports sections). No product perfectly serves the needs of **спортивные секции** — martial arts schools, swimming pools, dance studios, gymnastics clubs, etc. This creates a significant opportunity for Planovo.

### 1.1 SPORTLYZER (Estonia)

**Website:** sportlyzer.com  
**Founded:** 2012, Tartu, Estonia  
**Target Audience:** Youth & amateur sports clubs (football, swimming, gymnastics), coaches, club managers, athletes, parents

#### Key Features
- **Membership Management:** Centralized athlete database, profiles with personal data/contacts/emergency info/health info, import from Excel, training groups
- **Scheduling:** Team schedules & homework assignment, calendar sharing with players/parents/website, RSVP & attendance tracking
- **Payments:** Invoicing within platform, bank statement import, payment tracking, automatic payment reminders to debtors
- **Communication:** SMS, email, in-app notifications (report cards, RSVP requests, invoices, payment reminders)
- **Player Development:** Season plans with cycles/volume projection, daily/weekly planning, player evaluations & testing (objective/subjective/physical/non-physical), training feedback
- **Website Widgets:** Schedule widgets, membership registration forms, coach bios, free club profile page
- **Mobile Apps:** Separate apps for Coach, Player, and Manager — iOS & Android

#### UI/UX Patterns
- **Schedule View:** List-style schedule for each training group; calendar-based view for managers; players see their personal schedule
- **Dashboard:** Manager sees athlete count, payment status, upcoming events; Coach sees their groups & attendance
- **Mobile-first philosophy:** Dedicated apps per role (Coach app, Player app) — NOT a responsive web wrapper
- **Multi-role access:** Different data visibility for managers vs. coaches (managers can restrict coaches to only their training groups)
- **Languages:** Available in EN, ET, DE, LV, RU, SK, ES, CS — includes Russian!

#### Pricing Model
- **Free:** €0/month (limited features)
- **Basic:** ~€7.5/user or $29/month (for 50-200 athletes)
- **Price scales with number of athletes and coaches**
- SMS costs extra: €0.10 each
- Annual option ~€1,000/year (-30% discount)
- Custom website included in all plans

#### What They Do WELL
✅ Player development tracking is unique — no competitor offers season planning with volume projection  
✅ Separate mobile apps per role (Coach/Player/Manager) — excellent role-specific UX  
✅ Russian language support — already localized for CIS market  
✅ Attendance + RSVP workflow is well thought out  
✅ Invoice + payment tracking with bank statement import  
✅ Website widgets for schedule display (embeddable)  
✅ Free plan available for small clubs to start  

#### What They Do POORLY (Opportunities for Planovo)
❌ Very youth-sport focused — not designed for adult fitness sections, martial arts, dance  
❌ No facility/room booking — can't manage shared spaces (pools, halls, dojos)  
❌ No online payment processing (only invoicing + bank import) — no card payments  
❌ Pricing per athlete gets expensive fast for larger clubs  
❌ UI feels dated — Estonian-designed, not modern SaaS polish  
❌ No trial/free class booking — only full membership management  
❌ No group class capacity management with waitlists  
❌ Limited marketing tools — no promotional offers, intro deals

### 1.2 TEAMSNAP (USA)

**Website:** teamsnap.com  
**Founded:** 2009, Boulder, Colorado  
**Target Audience:** Youth sports teams & leagues (soccer, baseball, basketball, football), coaches, team managers, parents  
**Market Position:** #1 sports team management app by ratings (43,000+ reviews, 98% satisfaction)

#### Key Features
- **Team Management:** Roster management, player profiles, availability tracking, team chat
- **Scheduling:** Schedule maker, shared calendars, game & practice scheduling, RSVP
- **Communication:** Built-in team chat, messaging, notifications
- **Payments:** Payment tracking, registration & payment processing
- **Club/League Level:** Registration, club management, league management, tournament management
- **Content:** TeamSnap+ — expert-led practice plans, drills, activities
- **Live Streaming:** Built-in live streaming capability
- **Mobile Apps:** Full-featured iOS & Android apps (primary use case is mobile)

#### UI/UX Patterns
- **Mobile-first:** The app IS the product — most users never see desktop
- **Team-centric view:** Everything revolves around YOUR team — schedule, roster, chat, photos
- **Calendar is the hub:** Main screen shows upcoming games/practices with RSVP status
- **Tab navigation:** Schedule | Roster | Chat | Photos | Stats
- **Color-coded availability:** Green (attending), Red (declined), Gray (no response)
- **Notification-heavy:** Push notifications for schedule changes, RSVP reminders, messages

#### Pricing Model
- **Free:** Core features (team chat, scheduling) for small groups (<15 people), no availability tracking
- **Premium:** ~$120/year (~$10/month) per team — full features
- **Ultra:** ~$180/year per team — advanced features
- **Club/League:** Custom pricing, per-player (~$6.30/player for 750+ players)
- **Ads in free tier** — significant user complaint

#### What They Do WELL
✅ Best-in-class mobile experience — the gold standard for sports team apps  
✅ Extremely simple onboarding — create a team in 60 seconds  
✅ Communication is seamless — chat, notifications, RSVP all in one place  
✅ 98% satisfaction rate — users love it for what it does  
✅ Massive network effect — 24M+ users, parents already know TeamSnap  
✅ Practice plans & coaching content (TeamSnap+) adds value beyond scheduling  

#### What They Do POORLY (Opportunities for Planovo)
❌ ONLY team-focused — no facility management, no class booking, no individual training  
❌ No payment processing for classes/memberships — only registration fees  
❌ Not for sports SECTIONS — built for LEAGUE/TEAM sport (games, practices, tournaments)  
❌ Ads in free tier are annoying — even paid users complain  
❌ Per-team pricing is confusing and expensive for clubs with many teams  
❌ No attendance tracking for ongoing training sessions  
❌ No subscription/membership management — can't handle monthly payments  
❌ Not localized for Russian market at all  
❌ No facility/room scheduling

## 4. UI/UX PATTERN ANALYSIS

### 4.1 Schedule Display Patterns

| Pattern | Used By | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Weekly Grid (Timetable)** | Mindbody, YCLIENTS, Sport Priority, Fitbase | Mon-Sun columns, time rows, colored class blocks — the "fitness standard" |
| **Calendar View** | Sportlyzer, TeamSnap, SimplyBook | Month/week/day calendar with event blocks — general-purpose |
| **Team Schedule List** | TeamSnap, Sportlyzer | List of upcoming events for a specific team/group |
| **Facility Grid** | Playpass, 1С, YCLIENTS | Resources (rooms) on one axis, time on another — for multi-zone complexes |

### 4.2 Key UI/UX Insights for Planovo

**The Winning Pattern: Hybrid Schedule View**
- **Admin/Coach view:** Weekly timetable grid (like Mindbody/YCLIENTS) — this is what coaches and admins expect
- **Client/Parent view:** Simple list of "my upcoming sessions" with RSVP buttons (like TeamSnap)
- **Plus:** Calendar view option for those who prefer it

**Critical UX Elements:**
1. **One-tap RSVP** — TeamSnap proved this is essential
2. **Color-coded status** — Green/Yellow/Red for availability/full/waitlist
3. **Coach photo + name** on each class block — builds trust
4. **Capacity indicator** — "8/12 spots filled" — creates urgency
5. **Quick rebook** — "Same time next week?" one-tap confirmation

## 8. SUMMARY OF KEY FINDINGS

1. **The market is wide open** for a sports-section-specific product in Russia/CIS
2. **Western products** (Mindbody, TeamSnap, Sportlyzer) are too expensive, not localized, or not designed for the right use case
3. **Russian CRMs** (YCLIENTS, Fitbase, Mobifitness) are built for beauty salons or fitness studios, not спортивные секции
4. **Придёшка** is the closest competitor but lacks polish, payments, and market presence
5. **1С:Фитнес** is enterprise-only — completely inaccessible to small sections
6. **The #1 unmet need** is a product that handles: recurring schedule → auto-sessions → RSVP → attendance → абонемент deduction → parent notifications — all in one flow
7. **The #2 unmet need** is native Russian payment + messaging (Telegram/WhatsApp) integration
8. **The #3 unmet need** is modern, beautiful, mobile-first UI that coaches and parents actually WANT to use

**Planovo has a genuine "blue ocean" opportunity** to own the спортивная секция category in Russia/CIS by combining the best of Sportlyzer (player development, parent access), Mindbody (class scheduling, payments), and Придёшка (auto-scheduling, section focus) — with Russian market-native integrations and modern design.
